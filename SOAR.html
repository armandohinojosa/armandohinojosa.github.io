Service • Ownership • Achievement • Respect

S - Service

SERVICE means providing help or support to someone or something.

An example of service is helping someone with their groceries.

O - Ownership

OWNERSHIP means acknowledging and accepting responsibility for the consequences of your actions.

An example of ownership is admitting to making a mistake and taking the necessary steps to correct it, rather than blaming others or making excuses. For example, I broke a dish and acknowledged the mistake by saying it was me and apologizing.

A - Achievement

ACHIEVEMENT is the act of accomplishing something or achieving a goal.

An example of achievement is earning an honor roll certificate.

R - Respect

RESPECT means treating someone with consideration and esteem.

An example of respect is listening carefully to another person's opinions.
