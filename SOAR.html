SOAR Analysis

Service • Ownership • Achievement • Respect

Service

Definition: SERVICE means providing help or support to someone or something.

Personal Example: An example of service is helping someone with their groceries when they're struggling to carry multiple bags.

How I Apply Service

  • Helping classmates understand difficult technical concepts
  • Volunteering to assist with technology setup at school events
  • Supporting family members with computer troubleshooting
  • Contributing to community technology literacy programs

Ownership

Definition: OWNERSHIP means acknowledging and accepting responsibility for the consequences of your actions.

Personal Example: An example of ownership is admitting to making a mistake and taking the necessary steps to correct it, rather than blaming others or making excuses. For example, I broke a dish and acknowledged the mistake by saying it was me and apologizing.

My Ownership Mindset

  • Taking responsibility for project outcomes in group work
  • Acknowledging mistakes in code and learning from debugging
  • Being accountable for meeting deadlines and commitments
  • Owning my learning journey and actively seeking improvement

Achievement

Definition: ACHIEVEMENT is the act of accomplishing something or achieving a goal.

Personal Example: An example of achievement is earning an honor roll certificate for academic excellence.

My Key Achievements

  • Maintaining high academic performance throughout high school
  • Successfully completing complex programming projects
  • Developing strong technical and presentation skills
  • Building this comprehensive portfolio website
  • Earning work readiness certification

Respect

Definition: RESPECT means treating someone with consideration and esteem.

Personal Example: An example of respect is listening carefully to another person's opinions, even when you disagree with them.

How I Show Respect

  • Actively listening to teachers, peers, and family members
  • Valuing diverse perspectives in group discussions
  • Treating everyone with dignity regardless of differences
  • Respecting deadlines and other people's time
  • Honoring the expertise and experience of mentors

SOAR in My IT Career

These core values will guide my professional development and success as an IT System Administrator:

Service in IT

Providing exceptional technical support and solutions that help organizations succeed

Ownership in IT

Taking full responsibility for system reliability, security, and performance

Achievement in IT

Continuously earning certifications and delivering successful project outcomes

Respect in IT

Collaborating effectively with diverse teams and valuing every stakeholder's input
